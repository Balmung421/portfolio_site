Poetry Time by the

Campfire

The color of your fire will be different to those of your classmates.
Whatever color math decides for you, take it to heart and draft a poem
on how the fire makes you feel.
You have 10 min to draft, and 5 min to then read it aloud to yourself
or to others.

This fire was not made by me, but I wanted to see how it was made, since a piece of education tech
I am really interested in is the ability to use WebGL on the web. Once you draft a poem, I want you to be my classmate again and tell me how this could improve.
If you have any idea, model, or feeling you wanted to present this way, what would you want it to be?

Made with three.js by Jaume Sanchez.
